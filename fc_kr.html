About

Fruit Classifier is a computer vision system that uses machine learning algorithms to classify different types of fruits. By analyzing visual features and patterns, the system can accurately identify and categorize fruits based on their appearance. Fruit Classifier has applications in areas such as agriculture, inventory management, and food quality control.
