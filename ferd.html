About

FERD is an advanced system that utilizes computer vision techniques to analyze facial details and determine human emotions. By analyzing facial expressions, FERD can infer emotions such as happiness, sadness, anger, and surprise. This technology finds applications in fields like psychology, human-computer interaction, and market research.

Dataset:FER2013 (Facial Expression Recognition 2013 Dataset)
