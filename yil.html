About

yujin_ive_lora is a program designed to generate pictures of Yujin, who is an idol in the K-pop industry and a member of the South Korean girl group IVE. This program utilizes LoRA models, which are small Stable Diffusion models that apply subtle modifications to standard checkpoint models. LoRA models are known for their compact size, typically being 10 to 100 times smaller than traditional checkpoint models. This feature makes them particularly appealing to individuals who possess extensive collections of models.

yujin_ive_lora V2 will be launched in the first quarter of next year.
