About

Oureyes is an intelligent eyewear designed to assist individuals with disabilities. Incorporating computer vision and artificial intelligence technologies, Oureyes can recognize and interpret visual information to provide enhanced assistance to visually impaired individuals. The smart glasses can assist with object recognition, obstacle detection, and navigation, empowering users to navigate their surroundings more independently.
